About the D&D Initiative Tracker

This tool is designed to help Dungeon Masters and players manage the initiative order during their Dungeons & Dragons sessions. By tracking turns and ensuring everyone acts in the correct sequence, it streamlines gameplay and reduces confusion.

Our initiative tracker is easy to use and customizable, making it a valuable addition to any D&D game.
